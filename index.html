UCSD Dragon Boat Website


The UCSD Dragon Boat Team is a competitive dragon boat team at UC San Diego. Dragon Boat is an ancient canoeing sport originating from an annual ethnic ritual in southern China, that later spread into a competitive sport throughout the globe. A dragon boat is a 40 foot canoe with 20 paddlers (2 per row), a drummer, and a steersman. This website was created to showcase UCSD Dragon Boat and to spread word of dragon boat around the web. Logo
