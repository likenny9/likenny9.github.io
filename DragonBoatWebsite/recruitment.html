RECRUITMENT

OPEN PRACTICE

Open practices will be held all summer and at select times during the school year for anyone who is interested in trying out or simply wants to experience the sport. Please contact us for additional information or questions.

Practices are held weekly on Saturday and Sunday mornings at 9AM at Fiesta Island in Mission Bay.

Rides will be provided for students living on and off campus. On campus students meet at the turn-around by Center Hall and Town Square (at the umbrellas near Yogurtworld) at 8:30AM. Please email us at ucsddragonboat@gmail.com in advance to ensure that we can arrange a ride for you.

We also provide FREE equipment for those without their own, including boats, paddles, and personal floation devices (PFDs).
